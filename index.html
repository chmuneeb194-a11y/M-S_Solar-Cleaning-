Frequently Asked Questions

Sahiwal ke solar customers ke aam sawalaat aur unke tafseeli jawabaat.

Sahiwal ka zameeni paani (ground water) bohot khara hota hai aur usme high TDS hotay hain. Agar aap aam paani se panels dhoenge to un par safaid dhabbe (scaling) jamm jayegi, jo panel ki glass efficiency ko hamesha ke liye kam kar deti hai. Hum protective standards ka khyal rakhte hain.
Agar hamari safai ke baad agle 3 din ke andar Sahiwal mein achanak mitti ka tufan, aandhi, ya barish aa jaye jis se panels dobara gande ho jayen, to aapko agri wash par special flat 20% discount diya jayega taake aapka nuksaan na ho.
